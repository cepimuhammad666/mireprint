Get to Know Us !

Established in 1988 in Jakarta, PT Mire Prin Coll committed to deliver quality for your paper packaging needs. After 30 years of experience, we are still eagerly seeking for continuous improvements to give our partners an even better experience doing business with us.

We aim to have a long-lasting partnership by delivering great quality paper packaging with great service and competitive rates.

Technology

We believe technology is the game changing aspect in packaging industry. Hence, as we are committed to deliver only the best, we only utilize high-end printing equipments from Heidelberg (Made in Germany) for your packaging solutions. Having the resources, we have been delivering our partners great quality products in a timely manner and immaculate detail.
